# 🌱 Bloomstead

**Automate the rebirth of a world.**

![Bloomstead](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Bloodstead/.github/main/profile/bloomstead.png)

**Bloomstead** is a cozy 2D automation game where your factory doesn't pollute — it *heals*.
Harvest sap from crystals, chain extractors, belts and bloomers, and watch a withered world
turn green again, tile by tile, as your little production lines hum along.

Made with [Godot 4](https://godotengine.org) by a solo developer in France. Every sprite,
sound and song in the game is generated procedurally — no imported assets.

## ✨ What's inside

- 🏭 **9 machines** to combine — extractors, belts, splitters, tunnels, sprinklers, pumps…
- 🗺️ **24 handcrafted levels** + an endless mode and a daily challenge, across 9 biomes
- 🌸 **Progression that sticks** — permanent upgrades, prestige "Bloom Essence", 14 achievements
- 🧑‍🌾 **Free Garden** — a chill sandbox with decorations and a photo mode
- 👥 **Co-op** — rebuild the world with a friend
- 🌍 English & French, remappable keys, reduced-motion option, full touch support
